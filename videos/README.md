# Meet Me Video

## How to Add Your Video

1. Record a short "Meet Me" video (30-60 seconds recommended)
   - Introduce yourself
   - Talk about your Girl Scout journey
   - Explain why you're selling cookies
   - Keep it friendly and authentic!

2. Save your video as:
   - **Primary**: `meet-alice.mp4` (most compatible format)
   - **Optional**: `meet-alice.webm` (smaller file size)

3. **Video Specs (Recommended)**:
   - Format: MP4 (H.264 codec)
   - Resolution: 1280x720 (720p) or 1920x1080 (1080p)
   - Duration: 30-90 seconds
   - File size: Under 50MB (compress if needed)
   - Aspect ratio: 16:9 (horizontal) or 9:16 (vertical mobile)

4. **Optional: Add a Thumbnail**:
   - Save as: `images/video-thumbnail.jpg`
   - This shows before the video plays

## Tips for a Great Video

- Good lighting (natural light works best!)
- Clear audio (reduce background noise)
- Smile and be yourself
- Mention your cookie selling goal
- Keep it short and sweet

## Video Compression Tools

If your video is too large:
- **Online**: [HandBrake](https://handbrake.fr/) (free)
- **Mac**: QuickTime Player → Export → Reduce file size
- **Windows**: Windows Video Editor
- **Online**: [Clipchamp](https://clipchamp.com/)
