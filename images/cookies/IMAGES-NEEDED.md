## Where to get images:
- Official Girl Scout cookie website: https://www.girlscoutsrv.org/en/programs/cookies/meet-the-cookies.html
- Take photos of actual cookie boxes
- Use official Girl Scout marketing materials (with permission)

## Image specifications:
- Size: At least 600×600px each
- Format: JPG or PNG
- Aspect ratio: Square (1:1) preferred
